KT Space Agency (Kiatek Space Agency) is dedicated to bringing the latest and most exciting space news from Australia and around the globe. Our mission is to inspire and engage the Australian community in the growing space industry and promote innovation in space technology.


At KT Space Agency, we believe that Australia can lead the next wave of space exploration and innovation. We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date news and insights, fostering a culture of curiosity and discovery, and supporting the next generation of aerospace engineers and scientists.
